Document Services FAQ

Questions about passport, Aadhaar, PAN, certificates, and attestation services

How long does passport renewal take?

Regular passport renewal typically takes 7-15 working days through Tatkal and 15-30 days through normal process. With our expedited service, we aim to complete it in 5-10 working days depending on document verification and police verification status.

Normal: 15-30 days
Tatkal: 7-15 days
With us: 5-10 days

What documents do I need for Aadhaar update?

For Aadhaar updates, you need:

  • Proof of Identity: Passport, PAN card, Voter ID, Driving License
  • Proof of Address: Utility bill, bank statement, rental agreement
  • Proof of Date of Birth: Birth certificate, SSLC certificate
  • Current Aadhaar number and mobile number registered with Aadhaar

We help verify your documents before submission to avoid rejections.

Can you help with document attestation for foreign countries?

Yes, we provide complete document attestation services for all major countries including UAE, USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and European countries. Our process includes:

  1. Notary attestation
  2. Home Department/HRD attestation
  3. MEA (Ministry of External Affairs) attestation
  4. Embassy/Consulate attestation
  5. MOFA attestation (for Middle East countries)

Processing time: 10-25 working days depending on the destination country.

What is the difference between apostille and attestation?

Apostille is for Hague Convention countries (108 countries) and is a simplified certificate issued by MEA. Attestation is for non-Hague countries and involves embassy/consulate verification after MEA attestation.

Apostille (Hague Countries)

  • Single certificate from MEA
  • Simplified process
  • 108 member countries
  • Example: USA, UK, Australia

Attestation (Non-Hague)

  • MEA + Embassy attestation
  • Longer process
  • All other countries
  • Example: UAE, Saudi Arabia

What documents are needed for company bank account opening?

For opening a company bank account, you need:

Company Documents

  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • MOA & AOA
  • Board Resolution for account opening
  • PAN card of company

Director's Documents

  • PAN card of all directors
  • Aadhaar card of all directors
  • Address proof of all directors
  • Passport-size photographs

Business Proof

  • Business address proof
  • GST registration (if available)
  • Projected business plan

Do you help with income tax filing for individuals?

Yes, we provide complete income tax filing services for individuals, including:

  • ITR-1 (Sahaj): For salaried individuals with income from salary/pension, one house property, and other sources (excluding lottery winnings and race horses)
  • ITR-2: For individuals and HUFs not having income from profits and gains of business or profession
  • ITR-3: For individuals and HUFs having income from profits and gains of business or profession
  • ITR-4 (Sugam): For individuals, HUFs and Firms (other than LLP) having presumptive business income

We also help with tax planning, investment proof submission, and refund tracking.

What are the ongoing compliance requirements for a Private Limited company?

Private Limited companies have these annual compliance requirements:

Monthly

  • GST returns (if registered)
  • TDS/TCS returns
  • PF/ESI returns

Quarterly

  • Board meetings (min. 4 per year)
  • GSTR-3B & GSTR-1
  • TDS quarterly returns

Annual

  • Annual financial statements
  • Income tax return
  • MCA annual return (Form AOC-4 & MGT-7)
  • Audit report (if applicable)

Event-based

  • Change in directors
  • Change in registered office
  • Increase in share capital
  • Board resolutions filing

How long does US visa processing take?

US visa processing time varies by visa type:

Work Visa (H1B)

Processing time: 60-90 days

Premium processing: 15 calendar days (additional fee)
