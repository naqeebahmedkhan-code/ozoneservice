Passport & Visa FAQs

Common questions about travel documentation

1. How long does passport processing take?

Normal passport takes 15-30 days, Tatkal passport takes 2-3 working days after police verification (if applicable).

2. What documents are needed for USA tourist visa?

Passport, DS-160 confirmation, appointment letter, photographs, financial documents, employment proof, and travel itinerary.

3. Can I apply for passport if I don't have birth certificate?

Yes, you can submit alternative documents like school leaving certificate, PAN card, Aadhaar card, or affidavit in prescribed format.

4. What is Police Clearance Certificate (PCC)?

PCC is a document issued by police verifying that applicant has no criminal record. Required for employment, immigration, and long-term visas abroad.
