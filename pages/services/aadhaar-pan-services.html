Aadhaar & PAN FAQs

Common questions about identity document services

How long does it take to get a new Aadhaar card?

Typically 15-30 days after biometric enrollment. We track the status regularly and update you.

Can I update my Aadhaar details online?

Yes, for minor updates. However, major changes like name or date of birth require visiting Aadhaar center, which we can assist with.

What is the deadline for Aadhaar-PAN linking?

While there have been multiple deadlines, it's mandatory for tax filing. We recommend linking immediately to avoid issues.

Do you provide home/office service for Aadhaar?

Yes, we offer document pickup and delivery service for Aadhaar/PAN applications at additional convenience charges.

What if my name differs in Aadhaar and PAN?

We help correct the name in either document to match and then proceed with linking. This is a common issue we resolve.
