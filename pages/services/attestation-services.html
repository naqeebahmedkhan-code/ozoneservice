Attestation Services FAQs

Common questions about document attestation

What is the difference between attestation and apostille?

Apostille is for Hague Convention countries (108 countries) and is a simplified attestation. Non-Hague countries require regular embassy attestation after MEA attestation.

How long does document attestation take?

Typically 10-25 working days depending on document type, destination country, and government processing times. Urgent services available for faster processing.

Do you provide pickup and delivery service?

Yes, we offer document pickup and delivery service in Bengaluru. We can also arrange courier services for outstation clients.

Can I track my document status?

Yes, we provide regular updates on your document status through WhatsApp/email. You can also track through government portals where applicable.

What if my documents are rejected?

We thoroughly check documents before submission. In rare cases of rejection, we guide you on corrections and resubmit without additional service charges.
