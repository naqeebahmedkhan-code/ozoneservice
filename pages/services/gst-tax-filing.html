Tax FAQs

Common questions about GST and tax filing

1. Who needs GST registration?

Businesses with annual turnover exceeding ₹40 lakhs (₹20 lakhs for special category states), e-commerce sellers, interstate suppliers, and those required under reverse charge mechanism.

2. What are the penalties for late GST filing?

Late fee of ₹50 per day (₹25 CGST + ₹25 SGST) for each day of delay, maximum ₹5,000. Interest @18% per annum on tax due.

3. Can I file ITR myself or should I hire a professional?

While you can file yourself, professionals ensure accuracy, maximize deductions, handle complex cases, and provide audit support if needed.

4. How long does GST registration take?

Usually 3-7 working days after document submission, provided all documents are correct and there are no queries from department.
