Recharge & Utility FAQs

Common questions about our payment services

How quickly are recharges processed?

Mobile and DTH recharges are processed within 2-5 minutes. Utility bills take 15-30 minutes for confirmation.

Do you charge extra fees for bill payments?

No, we charge the exact bill amount without any extra fees. Our service is completely free for customers.

Can I pay bills after office hours?

Yes, we accept WhatsApp requests 24/7. Payments processed during next working hours if sent late night.

What if a recharge fails?

In case of failed transaction, we immediately refund the amount or reprocess as per your preference.

Do you provide payment receipts?

Yes, we provide digital receipts for all payments via WhatsApp or email for your records.
