Our Story Since 2006

For over 18 years, Ozone Service Solutions has been Bengaluru's trusted partner for professional document and business services.

Who We Are

Established in 2006, Ozone Service Solutions was founded with a simple mission: to make document processing and business services accessible, reliable, and hassle-free for everyone in Bengaluru.

What started as a small document assistance service in Jayanagar has grown into a comprehensive solutions provider, serving thousands of satisfied clients across the city.

Our Mission

To provide professional, efficient, and confidential services that simplify our clients' lives, saving them time and reducing the stress of government and legal procedures.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Honest and transparent in all our dealings
  • Reliability: Consistent, dependable service you can count on
  • Confidentiality: Complete privacy and security of your documents
  • Excellence: Commitment to quality in every service
  • Customer-Centric: Your satisfaction is our priority
