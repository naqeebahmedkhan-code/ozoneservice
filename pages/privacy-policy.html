This Privacy Policy describes how Ozone Service Solutions collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use our services, we may collect:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, postal address
  • Identification Details: Aadhaar number, PAN number, passport details, and other government ID information as required for service processing
  • Document Information: Copies of documents submitted for processing
  • Service Details: Information about the services you request from us
  • Communication Records: Correspondence via email, WhatsApp, phone calls, or in-person meetings

Non-Personal Information

  • Browser type and version
  • IP address and general location
  • Pages visited on our website
  • Time and date of visits

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To process and complete your document assistance requests
  • Communication: To provide updates, respond to inquiries, and send service-related information
  • Quality Improvement: To enhance our services and customer experience
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Security: To protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Marketing: To inform you about new services or promotions (with your consent)

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • Service Partners: With government authorities, banks, or third parties necessary to complete your requested services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulations
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Note: We only share the minimum necessary information required for service completion.

4. Data Security Measures

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

Physical Security

Secure storage of physical documents in locked cabinets

Digital Protection

Encrypted digital storage and secure transmission

Access Control

Limited access to authorized personnel only

Retention Policy

Regular secure disposal of unnecessary data

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Active Services: Information is retained during service processing and for a reasonable period afterward
  • Completed Services: Basic contact information may be retained for 3 years for customer service purposes
  • Document Copies: Copies of processed documents are securely disposed of after service completion unless required for legal purposes
  • Legal Requirements: Information required by law may be retained for specified periods

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

Request information about what data we have

Right to Correction

Request correction of inaccurate information

Right to Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information

Right to Object

Object to certain types of processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
